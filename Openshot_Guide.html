How to Install Openshot on your Chromebook

Description:

This guide will help you install Openshot on your Chromebook. Openshot is a basic open source video editor. This video editor works fairly well, though chrashing has been noted with large projects.

Requirements:

Recommended Requirements:

Press Ctrl+Search+Esc to find this:

  • 4GB+ RAM
  • Dual-core Intel or AMD CPU at a minimum of 2.50 GHz

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Linux VM set up, and updated
  • 8GB+ of free storage in Linux VM
  • 15 minutes of time

Directions:

Step 1: Open the terrminal and type in, "sudo apt-get upgrade -y" and press enter.

Tip: To paste commands just right-click the command line.

Step 2: Type in, "flatpak install flathub org.openshot.OpenShot -y" and press enter.

Step 3: Launch Openshot by pressing the search key ❪the one with a magnifying glass❫ and find an app called, "Openshot
